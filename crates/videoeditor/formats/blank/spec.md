# Format: blank

The unopinionated starting point: one title card, one content scene, one
scoreboard-style closer. No duel, no memes, no benchmark framing — use it
when your idea isn't an X-vs-Y matchup (an announcement, a tip, a story).

Everything is the same machinery as any other format: `[SCENE:]` picks a
template, `[DATA:]` feeds it, `[CLIP:]` narrates. Swap templates freely —
including your own (see docs/templates.md; `videoeditor pack init <episode>`
gives this video unique templates).

After `videoeditor tts`, heed the ⚠ fit-check warnings and recompute scene
durations: duration = clip `at` + measured audio + hold (PRODUCTION.md Rule 5).
